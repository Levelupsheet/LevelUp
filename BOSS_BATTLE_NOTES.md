Boss Battle System
- Session completion can now roll a boss encounter.
- Normal boss spawn probability is fixed at 12%.
- Golden boss spawn probability is fixed at 7%.
- Boss fights pull 3 high-difficulty questions from the active lane/set.
- Boss encounters are staged in BossEncounter and finalized by /api/game/boss-complete.
- Normal boss wins grant +100 bonus XP.
- Golden boss wins grant up to 3 raffle entries, capped by the existing weekly raffle limit.
- UI uses DiabloQuizRunner in boss mode with larger health pacing and golden visual treatment.
